Future Plans

Chasing dreams and shaping my destiny.

Become a Pilot

I aspire to become a pilot, following in the footsteps of my grandfather. Since childhood, I’ve been fascinated by large aircraft and the thrill of traveling to new places. I love meeting new people, embracing diverse cultures, and having a fresh challenge each day. Being 10 kilometers above sea level offers a sense of freedom that few other professions can match.

Study Political Science

I’ve always had a passion for political science and how it shapes our daily lives. Politics influences everything from taxes to personal freedoms, and by understanding this field, I can contribute to fair policies and help make decisions that affect entire communities. It’s a vital discipline that holds great responsibility, and I’m excited to dive deeper into its complexities.

Study Economic Science

Lastly, I plan to study economic science. Understanding how markets function will not only open many career opportunities but also empower me to make informed decisions in my own ventures. I want to use this knowledge to grow my business, scale up operations, and contribute to economic progress. By mastering economics, I hope to create real value and ensure long-term success.

These are the three ambitions I'm pursuing. Thank you for checking out my website!
